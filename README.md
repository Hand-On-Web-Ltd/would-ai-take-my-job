# Would AI Take My Job? 🤖

A fun, shareable quiz that tells you how likely AI is to replace your job. Pick your role, answer 6 quick questions about what you actually do day-to-day, and get a score from 0-100.

It's part joke, part reality check.

## Try It

Open `index.html` in your browser. That's it. No build step, no npm install, no frameworks.

Or host it anywhere — it's a single HTML page with some CSS.

## How It Works

1. Pick your job from the list (or type your own)
2. Answer 6 questions about your daily tasks
3. Get a score from 0 (you're safe) to 100 (start learning plumbing)
4. Share your result and watch your coworkers panic

## The Scoring

The quiz looks at things like:
- How much of your job is repetitive vs creative
- Whether you work with people face-to-face
- How much you rely on physical skills
- Whether your decisions need human judgment
- How much of your output is text/data based

It's not a scientific study — it's meant to make people think (and laugh).

## Hosting

Chuck it on GitHub Pages, Netlify, Vercel, or any static host. It's just HTML + CSS + JS in a single page.
